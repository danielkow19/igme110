Tired of Buggy Releases?

If so, many other players are feeling the same thing. Lots of people feel like they aren't getting complete games when buying on day one, or that games come out incomplete only to be finished with DLC or updates after the fact.

That's what it feels like to start losing trust in a company, and consumer trust can be one of the most powerful tools to a development team or publisher!

Building trust as a company helps both the players and the company itself.

For the players, it...

  • Makes more entertaining and satisfying products
  • Helps ensure a lack of gamebreaking bugs or missing features

For companies, it...

  • Allows for price increasing and minor slip-ups[source]
  • Makes consumers more lenient for future decisions

On INCOMPLETION, explore the realm of the good, the bad, and the trustworthy in the topic of game design and day one releases.
