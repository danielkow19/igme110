Works Cited

Browns, Ryan. "Mighty No. 9's Release Has Been One of Video Game's Biggest Catastrophes." Mirror Online, Daily Mirror, 22 June 2016, www.mirror.co.uk/tech/mighty-no-9s-release-been-8258577. Accessed 12 Sept. 2019. Summarizes the public backlash at the release of Mighty No. 9. Demonstrates how a game that feels unfinished or unpolished, especially given the hype and budget of the project, can completely tank consumer interest.

Delgado‐Ballester, Elena, and José Luis Munuera‐Alemán. "Brand Trust in the Context of Consumer Loyalty." European Journal of Marketing, vol. 35, no. 11/12, 1 Dec. 2001, pp. 1238-58. Emerald Insight, doi:10.1108/EUM0000000006475. Accessed 11 Sept. 2019. Details an experiment in brand trust and loyalty, specifically on its effects regarding consumer price leniency, or how willing a loyal customer is to pay more for a product.

Hamilton, Kirk. "Hollow Knight: The Kotaku Review." Kotaku, G/O Media, 5 July 2018, kotaku.com/hollow-knight-the-kotaku-review-1827367425. Accessed 12 Sept. 2019. A review of Hollow Knight, an example of a game that released in a satisfyingly complete state where the free downloadable content added later was a bonus, not a missing part of the core game.

Jackson, Gita. "No Man's Sky Is Good Now." Kotaku, G/O Media, 30 Aug. 2017, kotaku.com/no-mans-sky-is-good-now-1798630668. Accessed 11 Sept. 2019. A blog praising the changes to No Man's Sky, demonstrating how making the game more complete and fulfilling expectations greatly increases positive reception.

Kain, Erik. "The 5 Biggest Problems with 'Fallout 76.'" Forbes, Forbes Media, 5 Dec. 2018, www.forbes.com/sites/erikkain/2018/12/05/the-5-biggest-problems-with-fallout-76/#2b5510ef576e. Accessed 11 Sept. 2019. An article detailing the major problems with Fallout 76 at launch, most of which stem from a seeming lack of polish and completeness. Provides an example of an unfinished game from a large, well known franchise financially failing.

Marshall, Cass. "Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Ad Hints That Stage Builder Mode Is Coming Soon." Polygon, Vox Media, 9 Apr. 2019, www.polygon.com/2019/4/9/18303962/super-smash-bros-ultimate-stage-builder-tease-leak. Accessed 12 Sept. 2019. An article discussing the introduction of a stage builder mode in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, a feature present in previous games in the series that was missing at launch for the newest entry.

"ファミ通チャンネル特番～ファミ通アワード2018授賞式～." Youtube, uploaded by ファミ通TUBE, Google, 5 Apr. 2019, www.youtube.com/watch?v=SVM3Q37mzJ0. Accessed 12 Sept. 2019. The archived stream of the Famitsu Game Awards 2018 Ceremony. Announces Super Smash Bros. Ultimate as their game of the year.
